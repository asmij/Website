I'm a user experience designer in Seattle, Washington. I'm passionate about creating memorable user experiences, and I'm a strong advocate of informed design.

I am a first-year Masters student in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington. Currently I am conducting user research for the Civic Action Challenge for Puget Soundoff. I am also TAing for CSEP 510, an intro to HCI course for the professional masters program in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the UW.

I am interested in finding an internship opportunity that will further my goals of becoming a UX designer by expanding and enriching my experience in understanding user behavior and performing user research in order develop memorable experiences.
